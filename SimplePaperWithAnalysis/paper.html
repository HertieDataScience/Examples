Explanation

I gathered data from Pemstein, Meserve, and Melton (2010) on countries’ democracy level. They call their democracy measure the Unified Democracy Score (UDS). I also gathered data from the World Bank (2013) on fertilizer consumption.1 Figure 1 shows the mean UDS scores over time for all of the countries in their sample plotted against the natural logarithm of their fertilizer consumption.
